## Choose Your Faction

<div class="space-y-8 mb-10">

### United States Forces

<div class="space-y-4 mb-8">

**Firepower & Flexibility**

The US faction excels at combined arms warfare with strong infantry, versatile armor, and overwhelming artillery support. American doctrines emphasize mobility, late-game scaling, and the ability to adapt to any battlefield situation.

**Faction Strengths:**
- Elite infantry options across multiple doctrines
- Strong medium armor (Sherman variants)
- Excellent artillery and air support capabilities
- Powerful off-map abilities

**Available Doctrines:** Airborne Company, Armor Company, Heavy Cavalry Company, Infantry Company, Mechanized Company, Rifle Company

[View USA Doctrines →](/company-of-heroes-2/usa)

</div>

### Soviet Union

<div class="space-y-4 mb-8">

**Mass & Momentum**

Soviet forces leverage numerical superiority, versatile conscripts, and devastating late-game armor. Their doctrines range from defensive fortification to overwhelming assault, with unmatched artillery options and the most feared heavy tanks in the game.

**Faction Strengths:**
- Cost-effective infantry swarms
- Most heavy armor options (IS-2, ISU-152, KV-series)
- Powerful rocket artillery
- Strong defensive and offensive doctrines

**Available Doctrines:** Guards Rifle Division, Shock Rifle Division, Tank Guards Division, Airborne Guards Division (ODON), Rifle Guards Division, Artillery Guards Division

[View Soviet Doctrines →](/company-of-heroes-2/soviet)

</div>

### Wehrmacht

<div class="space-y-4 mb-8">

**Precision & Discipline**

The Wehrmacht combines tactical flexibility with devastating firepower. German doctrines emphasize defensive superiority, elite infantry, and the most feared anti-tank capabilities in the game, backed by strong combined arms options.

**Faction Strengths:**
- Superior defensive capabilities
- Elite infantry (Panzergrenadiers, Stormtroopers)
- Best anti-tank weapons and armor
- Excellent combined arms synergy

**Available Doctrines:** Panzer-Lehr Division, Fallschirmjäger-Division, Luftwaffe-Feld-Division, Panzergrenadier-Division, Elefant-Jagdpanzer-Division, Festung-Panzer-Division

[View Wehrmacht Doctrines →](/company-of-heroes-2/wehrmacht)

</div>

### British Forces

<div class="space-y-4 mb-8">

**Defensive Excellence & Artillery Dominance**

British forces excel at holding ground with superior defensive emplacements and the game's most devastating artillery. Their doctrines emphasize positional warfare, infantry sections with powerful upgrades, and the ability to punish aggressive opponents.

**Faction Strengths:**
- Best defensive emplacements
- Superior artillery options
- Strong infantry with multiple upgrade paths
- Excellent team weapon support

**Available Doctrines:** Guards Armoured Regiment, Royal Marine Commando Regiment, Special Weapons Regiment, Royal Artillery Support Regiment, Royal Engineers Regiment, Commando Regiment

[View British Doctrines →](/company-of-heroes-2/uk)

</div>

### Waffen-SS (Oberkommando West)

<div class="space-y-4 mb-8">

**Elite Forces & Aggressive Tactics**

The Waffen-SS faction features the game's most elite infantry and specialized tactical options. Their doctrines emphasize quality over quantity, with powerful specialist units, aggressive tactics, and unique abilities unavailable to other factions.

**Faction Strengths:**
- Most elite infantry in the game
- Specialized tactical units
- Strong early-to-mid game aggression
- Unique abilities and call-ins

**Available Doctrines:** Luftwaffe Jäger Tactics, Waffen Grenadier Tactics, SS Mechanized Tactics, SS Jäger Tactics, Volkssturm Tactics, Festung Panzer Tactics

[View Waffen-SS Doctrines →](/company-of-heroes-2/oberkommando)

</div>

</div>

## Faction Balance Overview

<div class="space-y-6 mb-8">

Understanding how each faction approaches key battlefield roles helps inform doctrine selection and overall strategy. Here's how the factions compare across critical categories:

</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6 mb-8">

<div class="p-6 rounded-lg border-2 border-primary/20 bg-card/30">

**Elite Infantry Access**

- **UK:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">3 doctrines</span> (Guards, Commandos, Royal Marines)
- **USSR:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">3 doctrines</span> (Guards, Shock, Airborne)
- **USA:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">2 doctrines</span> (Airborne, Rifle)
- **Wehrmacht:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">2 doctrines</span> (Fallschirmjäger, Panzergrenadier)
- **SS:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">1 doctrine</span> (SS Jäger)

</div>

<div class="p-6 rounded-lg border-2 border-primary/20 bg-card/30">

**Artillery Dominance**

- **All Factions:** Heavy artillery presence
- **USSR:** Most rocket artillery options
- **UK:** Best static artillery
- **USA:** Superior off-map artillery
- **Wehrmacht/SS:** Precision strike capabilities

</div>

<div class="p-6 rounded-lg border-2 border-primary/20 bg-card/30">

**Heavy Armor Availability**

- **USSR:** Most heavy tanks (IS-2, ISU-152, KV-series)
- **Wehrmacht:** Super-heavy tank destroyers (Elefant)
- **USA:** Heavy Pershing and Sherman variants
- **UK:** Churchill variants, limited heavy options
- **SS:** Limited heavy armor, focus on medium tanks

</div>

<div class="p-6 rounded-lg border-2 border-primary/20 bg-card/30">

**Air Support Access**

- **USSR:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">3 doctrines</span> with significant air power
- **Wehrmacht:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">2 doctrines</span> (Luftwaffe focus)
- **SS:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">2 doctrines</span> (Luftwaffe Jäger)
- **USA:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">1 doctrine</span> (Airborne)
- **UK:** <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">1 doctrine</span> (limited)

</div>

</div>

<Callout variant="warning">
**Doctrine Tags & Meta:** Each doctrine is tagged with key attributes (Artillery, Anti-Tank, Heavy Armor, Mobility, Elite Infantry, Defense, Air Power). Understanding these tags helps you counter enemy strategies and exploit weaknesses.
</Callout>

### Aim for Upper Chest, Not Head

Targeting the upper chest/collarbone area naturally leads to headshots due to recoil climb. This technique improves your effective accuracy while maintaining lethal headshot potential‚Äîespecially important for weapons with headshot multipliers like the M4A1 with Hollow Point.

### Reload Management is Critical

High fire rate weapons (KV-9, M4A1, TR-7) empty magazines faster than you expect. Develop muscle memory for:
- Reloading between engagements, not during
- Using cover when reloading
- Recognizing when Fast Mags are worth the attachment points

### Class Synergy Matters

While Carbines work with any class, consider strategic pairings:
- **M4A1 + Assault**: Dual primary setup (M4A1 + Shotgun sling)
- **DRS-IAR + Support**: Ammo crate for sustained fire
- **Mini Scout + Recon**: Motion sensors and spawn beacons for aggressive sniping

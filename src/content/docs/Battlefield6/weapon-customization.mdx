## The Pick 100 Attachment System

<div class="space-y-6 mb-8">

Battlefield 6 revolutionizes weapon customization with the Pick 100 system‚Äîa point budget that forces meaningful strategic choices. Primary weapons receive exactly <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">100 attachment points</span> to spend across all modification categories, while secondary weapons get <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">60 points</span>. Each attachment carries a specific point cost, and you must stay within your budget to deploy your loadout.

    <p>The 120-second cooldown enables <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">strategic layering</span>—deploying multiple minefields over the course of a mission to create persistent area denial. This wasn't possible with the old 180s cooldown.</p>
    <p>Perhaps most importantly, mines shift the difficulty curve: they reward <span class="text-fs-gold font-semibold">tactical planning over mechanical skill</span>. A well-placed minefield achieves what rapid reflexes and perfect aim struggle to accomplish—controlling space and forcing enemy behavior.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<Callout variant="tip">
**For players new to mines:** Start with gas mines on bug missions. They're the most forgiving (won't instantly kill friendlies), provide immediate visual feedback (gas clouds), and offer the most versatility. Once comfortable with placement and communication, add AP or AT mines to your loadout.
</Callout>

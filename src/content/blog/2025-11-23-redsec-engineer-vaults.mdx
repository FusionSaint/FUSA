### Step 2: Rotate to Lyndon Oilworks (Vault B)

<div class="space-y-4 mb-8">

Grab a vehicle from the Chemical parking lot and drive south. The Oilworks vault is located in the central office building.

*   **Threat Check:** This is a B-Tier POI, so you might bump into one other squad. Use your superior gear from Vault A to wipe them.
*   **The Prize:** More S-Tier weapons and, crucially, **Vehicle Keycards** for late-game tank access.

</div>

### Step 3: End at Redline Storage (Vault C)

<div class="space-y-4 mb-8">

Finish the loop at the warehouses. The vault here is often overlooked because it's tucked behind the shipping containers.

*   **The Payoff:** By this point, you should have multiple S-Tier weapons and full armor plates for the squad. You are now ready to hunt High Value Assets (HVAs) while the rest of the lobby is still scraping for loot.

## <Lock className="inline-block mr-3 mb-1" size={32} /> The Mechanics: How to Open Vaults

<div class="space-y-6 mb-8">

Many new Engineers don't realize how the mechanic works and get shot while fumbling with the door.

1.  **Equip Repair Tool:** Press `3` (default) to pull it out.
2.  **Interact:** Hold the interaction key on the vault mechanism.
3.  **Don't Stop:** You must complete the full progress bar. If you let go, it resets.
4.  **Time:** It takes **several seconds** to open a vault.

</div>

<Callout variant="warning">
**The Risk:** You are stationary and defenseless while opening the vault. Ensure your squad has your back before you start the unlock process.
</Callout>

<div class="my-12 border-t-2 border-primary/30"></div>

## <Shield className="inline-block mr-3 mb-1" size={32} /> Squad Protocol: Protect the Key

<div class="space-y-6 mb-8">

The Engineer is vulnerable while working. Do not let them do it alone.

*   **Assault:** Hold the door/corridor. You are the first line of defense.
*   **Recon:** Place a Motion Sensor outside the building *before* the unlock starts.
*   **Support:** Drop ammo on the Engineer. They might need it immediately after the door opens if there's a fight.

</div>

<div class="my-12 border-t-2 border-primary/30"></div>

## <Users className="inline-block mr-3 mb-1" size={32} /> Loot Distribution Priority

<div class="space-y-6 mb-8">

Don't be a loot goblin. Distribute the S-Tier gear to maximize squad efficiency.

1.  **S-Tier ARs/SMGs:** Go to your **Assault** and **Engineer**. They are your fraggers.
2.  **S-Tier Snipers:** Go to **Recon** (obviously).
3.  **Armor Plates:** Priority to the **Support** (the Anchor). If they die, the squad wipes.
4.  **Vehicle Keycards:** Give these to the **Engineer**. They are the driver/repair specialist.
5.  **Upgrade Kits:** Use these immediately on any non-S-Tier weapons you plan to keep.

</div>

<Callout variant="success">
**Final Tip:** Always prioritize **Class Chests** over standard loot if you see them. They contain specialized gear tailored to your role, which complements the raw firepower found in vaults.
</Callout>
